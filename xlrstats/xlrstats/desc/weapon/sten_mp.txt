Sten Mark II<br>
The Sten sub-machinegun was the standard sub-machinegun of the British forces during WWII. Over 2 million were made in three years. I was easily dismantled and ideal for clandestine operations in occupied Europe.
<br><br>

Calibre: 9mm Parabellum<br>
Magazine: Clip 32
