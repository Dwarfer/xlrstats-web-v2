Luger P08 (Pistole 1908)<br>
Luger was one of the earliest self-loading pistol designs. It was adopted by the German Army in WWI and used through WWII. Thousands were made during that time. In 1938 the P-38 was adopted, designed to be a simpler, more rugged replacement for the Luger.

Calibre: 9mm Parabellum<br>
Magazine: Clip 8
