Webley MK IV 38<br>
The Webley revolver, MK IV was very similar in design to the Enfield Mk II revolver. The Webley fits in the airborne issued Tanker holster as well as the standard P37 web holster.
<br><br>

Calibre: .380in British Service<br>
Chamber: 6 rounds
