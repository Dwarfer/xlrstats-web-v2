BAR M1918A2 Browning Automatic Rifle<br>
The Browning Automatic Rifle was developed too late for front-line service on any large scale in WWI. The weapon served as a light machine-gun through WWII. It is heavy and has limited magazine capacity, but is very robust and reliable.
