Raketenpanzerbuechse Panzerschrek<br>
The Panzerschreck was a German copy of the US bazookas which were captured in North Africa. Its 88mm rocket contained a hollow charge ezplosive, and while not as effective as the US bazooka, it was far better than the anti-tank rifles it replaced.
