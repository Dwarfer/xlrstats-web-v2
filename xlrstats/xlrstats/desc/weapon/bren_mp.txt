Bren LMG<br>
The BREN machine gun had been initially developed in Czechoslovakia as ZB-26, and then redesigned to fire rimmed British .303 ammunition. The designation BREN stands for (BRno-ENfield). Manufacture of the Bren Mk.1 began in Enfield (UK) in 1937 and saw wide and successful use during the World WWII.
<br><br>

Calibre: 303 British<br>
Magazine: Clip 30
