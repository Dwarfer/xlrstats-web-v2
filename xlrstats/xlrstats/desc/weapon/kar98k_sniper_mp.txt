KAR-98K / KAR-98K (Scoped)<br>
Standard German rifle of WWII. The Kar 98k appeared in 1935 and was prodused throughout the war. It was the last Mauser bolt action military rifle produced and was known for its reliability.
<br><br>
Calibre: 7,92 Mauser 7,92 x 57 mm (8 x 57 mm IS)<br>
Magazine: Clip 5
