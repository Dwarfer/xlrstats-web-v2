Lee Enfield No. 4<br>
The Rifle Number 4 was similar to the Short Magazine Lee Enfield used in WW I. It was designed for wartime production and was used throughout WW II. Some of these rifles were made in the USA and in Canada.

Calibre:.303 British<br>
Magazine: Clip 10
