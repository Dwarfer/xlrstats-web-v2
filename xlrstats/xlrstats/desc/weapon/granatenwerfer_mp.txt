Granatenwerfer 34<br>
The Granatenwerfer was the German equivalent motar of the U.S. 81mm M-1. It fired both explosive and smoke grenades. After 1943 when the 12cm (120 mm) Schwerer (Heavy) mortar was introduced, it took on the new designation of Medium launcher.
<br><br>

Type: smooth-bore, muzzle-loaded medium mortar<br>
Caliber:   81.4mm
