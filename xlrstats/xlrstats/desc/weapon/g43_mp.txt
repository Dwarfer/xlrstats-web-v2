Gewehr 43<br>
The Gewehr 43 replaced the Gewehr 41 and was generally used for sniping. it was encountered in smal numbers on both fronts,and always as a specialist's weapon. The Gewehr 43 had a number of improvements over the Gewehr 41, including a removeable box magazine.
<br><br>

Calibre: 7,92 Mauser 7,92 x 57 mm (8 x 57 mm IS)<br>
Magazine: Clip 10
