Nambu<br>
The Nambu pistol was a semi-automatic pistol used by the Imperial Japanese Army and Navy during the First and Second World Wars. The pistol had two variations, the Type A (also called the Type 4), and the Type 14
<br><br>

Cartrige: 8x22mm Nambu<br>
