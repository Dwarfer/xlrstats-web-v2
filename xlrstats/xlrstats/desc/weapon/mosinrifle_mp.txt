Mosin Nagant<br>
The Mosin-Nagant (Russian: Vintovka Mosina) is a bolt-action, internal magazine fed, military rifle that was used by the armed forces of the Russian Empire, the Soviet Union and various Eastern bloc nations. Also known as the Three-Line Rifle, it was the first to use the 7.62x54mmR cartridge.
<br><br>

Cartrige: 7.62x54mmR<br>
Feed system: 5-round magazine, loaded with five-round charger clips
